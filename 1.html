Rajkiya Engineering College, Bijnor

Rajkiya Engineering College (REC), Bijnor, is an autonomous government engineering college established in 2010 under the Special Component Plan by the Government of Uttar Pradesh. It aims to promote technical education among socially and economically weaker sections. The college is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and approved by AICTE. REC Bijnor offers B.Tech programs in various disciplines, with admissions based on JEE Main scores.

Facilities

  • Campus: Spanning 5.64 acres with modern infrastructure, including academic buildings, laboratories, and a computer center with high-speed internet.
  • Library: Over 7,600 books and access to digital resources like DELNET.
  • Hostel: Six hostels with facilities for boys and girls, mess services, and 24-hour internet.
  • Sports: Indoor and outdoor sports facilities for cricket, football, chess, and more.

Fee Structure

  • Annual tuition: ₹40,000
  • Hostel Fee: ₹10,000/year
  • Exam Fees: ₹7,500/year
